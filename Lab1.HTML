Project Description:

The project will be a hospital management system where the site can be assessed by patients and the doctors. Patients will be able to see their upcoming surgeries and appointments along with information about the medication they are prescribed. The doctors/staff will be able to add/remove patients and will also be able to edit all the information that the patients can view. Both entities will be able to view/change respective information such as, phone number, address, DOB etc... The patients will be able to request their appointments to be rescheduled and will also have the ability to cancel their appointments. The doctors/staff will be able access and respond to these requests/changes.
