Project Description:

The project will be a sports team management system where the site can be assessed by players and the coach of the team. Users will be able to see their upcoming games as well as the results of previous games. The coach will be able to add or drop players from their team. All users will be able to see their roster as well as various statistics related to the team such as player sattas and team record. The coach will be able to access the teams finances. The players will be able to mark their availability for upcoming games and the users will be able to see this information.
